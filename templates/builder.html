Getting Started

  1. Sign in with Patreon. Click Sign in with Patreon in the builder to authenticate.
  2. Open the {{module.label}} tab at the top of this window to reach the form.
  3. Describe your {{documentNoun}}. Fill in a name, level, and description — the more detail you provide, the better the result.
  4. Click Generate. Your new {{documentNoun}} is added to Foundry and appears in the history panel on the right.

Click any past entry in the history panel to re-populate the form and tweak it.

You're using {{module.label}}. The CferNpcMaker family ships a separate Foundry module for each system so each install only contains the code it needs. Install any of the others to round out your toolset.
